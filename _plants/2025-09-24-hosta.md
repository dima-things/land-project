# Hosta

aka funkia. 

I see these all over my city. 

Flowering plant, but I'm mostly interested for their lovely and plentiful medium sized foliage. Nice greens and shade tolerant.  

Doesn't grow so high, maybe 1 foot, so excellent for ground cover.
